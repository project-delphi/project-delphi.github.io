Hello there!

I am an applied ML research scientist, my interests are now in BioTech applications for start ups. I have experience with a wide repertoire of ML/DL (Computer Vision & Generative NLP) techniques in business/tech, Cloud Computing, Web Development and MLOps/DevOps Projects using AGILE/SCRUM methods.

I love modern programming practice for scientific applications and speak internationally at conferences on the modern functional programming paradigm which creates clean, clear & concise computational prose. As a machine learning enthusiast I developed the ability to create valuable ML products suitable to market opportunities.

I enjoy working in groups with developers, pair programming, implementing, presenting and sharing good practice. If you need to contact me, email (good for FaceTime also), Twitter and LinkedIn - in that order are usually the best ways to get in touch. Links on this page.
